REQUIREMENTS & WARNINGS

MEDICAL CLEARANCE

All participants must provide a medical certificate from a licensed physician confirming fitness for high-altitude or extreme environment activities.

INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS

Comprehensive travel and medical insurance is mandatory for all expeditions, with minimum coverage of $100,000 for medical evacuation.

DOCUMENTATION NEEDED

Valid passport with at least 6 months validity, visa approvals where required, and emergency contact information must be submitted.

FITNESS & EXPERIENCE

Participants must be in excellent physical condition and have relevant experience for the chosen expedition's difficulty level.
